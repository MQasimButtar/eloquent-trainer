Turn this web app into a full iPhone app using PWA technology

ℹ️
What is a PWA? A Progressive Web App lets you install any website to your iPhone home screen. It runs fullscreen like a native app and costs nothing.
Method 1 — Safari "Add to Home Screen" (Easiest)
1
Open Eloquent in Safari on your iPhone
Important: must be Safari. Go to this URL:

Tap the Share button
At the bottom of Safari, tap the square with an arrow pointing up (⬆).
3
Select "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen". Give it a name and tap Add.
4
Launch from Home Screen
Eloquent now appears on your home screen like a real app!
